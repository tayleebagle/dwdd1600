Cooking Beginners Guide

This website is designed to provide access to simple recipes for anyone who likes to or is learning to cook. You will find basic information below and on each page there is different recipes. We hope you enjoy making these recipes!

Liquid Measuring Conversions

  • 1 fluid ounce = 2 tablespoons
  • 1 cup = 8 fluid ounces
  • 1 pint = 2 cups or 16 fluid ounces
  • 1 quart = 2 pints or 32 fluid ounces
  • 1 gallon = 4 quarts or 128 fluid ounces

Dry Measuring Conversions

  • 1 tablespoon = 3 teaspoons
  • 1/4 cup = 4 tablespoons
  • 1/3 cup = 5 tablespoons + 1 teaspoon
  • 1/2 cup = 8 tablespoons
  • 1 cup = 16 tablespoons

Abbreviations

  • t or tsp = teaspoon
  • T, TB or tbsp = tablespoon
  • C or c = cup
  • pt = pint
  • qt = quart
  • gal = gallon
  • oz = ounce
  • fl oz = fluid ounce
  • lb = pound

Definitions

Bake

Beat
Mixing rapidly while incorporating some amount of air into the substance.
Boil
Heating a liquid until it bubbles.
