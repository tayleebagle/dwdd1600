Broccoli Cheese Soup

Ingredients
4 cups cubed potatoes
2 cups chopped celery
2 cups diced carrots
1/2 cup diced onions
2 tsp salt
4 cups water
10 oz frozen broccoli
2 TB chicken base(or 3 chicken boullion cubes)
1/2 cup maragarine
1/2 cup flour
1 TB dry mustard
1 lb velveeta cheese

Directions

  1. Cook together potatoes, celery, carrots, onions, salt, and water until tender.
  2. Then add frozen broccoli and chicken base.
  3. In another pan melt margarine and add flour and dry mustard.
  4. Add the second mixture to the first and stir until thickened.
  5. Cut up velveeta cheese and add to soup.
  6. Stir until melted.

Peach Jello Salad

Ingredients
1 large package of peach jello
1 container whipped cream
1 can of peaches

Directions

  1. Follow instructions on jello box but use 1/2 cup less liquid.
  2. Get the jello 3/4 of the way set.
  3. Whip jello and whipped cream
  4. Drain peaches and the fold in with the mixture.
