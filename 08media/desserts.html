Fudge Bars

Ingredients
Fudge Bars
1 c butter
2 c brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 c flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
3 c oatmeal

Fudge
12 oz chocolate chips
1 can eagle milk
2 T butter
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Cream together 1 cup of butter and brown sugar.
  2. Add 2 eggs, 2 tsp vanilla, 2 1/2 cups flour, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp baking soda, and 3 cups oatmeal.
  3. Put 2/3 of the dough on a cookie sheet.
  4. Melt together chocoalte chips, eagle milk, 2 T butter, 2 tsp vanilla, 1/2 tsp salt.
  5. Pour over dough in the pan.
  6. Crumble remaining 1/3 of dough over the top.
  7. Bake on 350° for 15-20 minutes.

Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients
1 c butter
2 c brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 c flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
3 c oatmeal
2/3 bag chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Cream together 1 cup of butter and brown sugar.
  2. Add 2 eggs, 2 tsp vanilla, 2 1/2 cups flour, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp baking soda, and 3 cups oatmeal.
  3. Add chocolate chips.
  4. Roll dough into balls and put on a cookie sheet.
  5. Bake on 350° for 12 minutes.
