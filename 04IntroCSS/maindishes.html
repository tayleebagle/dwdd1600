Main Dishes

Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients
1 lb hamburger
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 can of milk
1 can mushrooms
1 T Worcestershire sauce
1 t garlicsalt
1 T dreid onion
1 bag wide egg noodles

Directions

  1. Cook hamburger until brown in a saucepan.
  2. Then combine hamburger, cream of mushroom soup, milk, mushrooms, Worcestershire sauce, galric salt, and dried onions.
  3. Let the sauce simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Cook egg noodles with the directions on the bag.
  5. Once the sauce and noodles are done serve the sauce over the noodles.
