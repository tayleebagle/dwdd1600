Seven Layer Dip

Ingredients
2 small or 1 large can of refried beans
1 jar of salsa
1 cubed green pepper
2 cans of green chilis
1 can sliced olives
2-3 cut up tomatoes
1/2 container Dean's Guacamole
Sour Cream
Grated Cheese

Directions

  1. Put on layer of refried beans in a 9x11 cake pan.
  2. Then add a layer of salsa, a layer of Dean's Guacamole, and a layer of sour cream in the pan.
  3. Sprinkle on the green pepper, green chilis, sliced olives, and cut up tomatoes.
  4. Add a layer of grated cheese to the top.
  5. Bake on 350Â° for 7 minutes and serve with tortilla chips
