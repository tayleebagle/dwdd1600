Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients
1/4 onion finely chopped
1/2 cup butter-melted
3T bread crumbs
1/4 tsp. dry mustard
1 can minced clams drained
1 pound large fresh mushrooms

Directions

  1. Combine onion, 1/4 cup melted butter, bread crumbs, & mustard.
  2. Stir in clams & set asside.
  3. Discard mushroom stems.
  4. Put mushroom caps on baking sheet round side up. Brush tops with 1/4 cup melted butter.
  5. Broil until lightly brown-Flip & fill with mixture.
  6. Bake on 325Â° for 7-8 minutes
